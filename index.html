3 Broken Heart Quotes to Help You Heal

Breaking up is hard to do, cue all the weepy heartbreak songs and especially those country heartbreak songs! To help you recover and survive the healing journey (which can take months!), we've rounded up 3 broken heart quotes to lift your spirits and bring comfort to your healing heart..

"A slow healing heart dying to mend. Longin' for love, lonely again. But when the spirit is broken and the memories start. Nothing moves slower than a slow healing heart" -Dolly Parton

"God bless the girls. Who bring home the wine. When you need a friend, no, they don't think twice. They let you go on for the ten-thousandth time. And tell the same stories about the same guy. They're easy to keep. And harder to find. God bless thе girls. And thank God for mine" -Megan Moroney

“It is only with true love and compassion that we can begin to mend what is broken in the world. It is these two blessed things that can begin to heal all broken hearts.” -Steve Maraboli
